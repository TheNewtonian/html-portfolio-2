The Best Anime Series According to Pasteur

My top 3 anime series of all time

Naruto

Such a perfect anime and a huge success in the shonen genre of anime. All over the world, it is one of the most classic examples of what anime should be and has become an inspiration for numerous anime series. It has always been considered as the best for anime beginners.

Jujutsu Kaisen (JJK)

If you're a fan of action-packed anime, then I'd recommend watching it. The storyline and plot are crazy while the characters are relatable and lovable to true anime lovers around the globe. I 100% recommend it!

Demon Slayer (Kimetsu no Yaiba)

As the name implies, it's an anime series on slaying demons. It goes hard on many levels and the main character could become a true life inspiration. It's very relatable and teaches some lessons if you are into deep stuff. It is action-packed as well and is a great watch.
